Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

At pekelund.dev, we value your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to improve your experience on our website:

  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information that is automatically collected when you visit the website.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with the website, which pages you visit, and how long you stay.
  • Contact information: If you choose to contact us via email, we save your email address and the content of your message to respond to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our website and its content
  • To analyze usage patterns to optimize the user experience
  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you
  • To display relevant ads via Google AdSense

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your experience.

Google AdSense and Cookies: We use Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google's ad settings.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Necessary cookies: Required for the website to function properly.
  • Analytical cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the website.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant ads.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdSense: To display ads. Read Google's privacy policy for more information.
  • GitHub Pages: Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Read GitHub's privacy policy for more information.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following situations:

  • With service providers who help us operate the website
  • If required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request correction of incorrect data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to object to processing of your data
  • Right to data portability

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We recommend that you regularly review this policy.
